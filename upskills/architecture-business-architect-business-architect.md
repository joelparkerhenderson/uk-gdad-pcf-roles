
# Business architect - upskill resources

This page focus on learning and growth resources for the work role of business architect.

- The resources include links to online courses, blogs, research articles, videos, and books. 

- These help with job upskilling, work training, professional development, and career advancement,

- These are intended to be good starting points, not a comprehensive catalogue.

We welcome constructive feedback to improve this page and these lists.

- GitHub issues and/or pull requests are welcome.

- The resources are found via AI searches then go through human-in-the-loop proofing.

- For more contact Joel Henderson <joel@joelparkerhenderson.com>

## Coursera courses

- [TOGAF 10 Foundation](https://www.coursera.org/learn/togaf-10-foundation): This comprehensive course provides an exploration of enterprise architecture fundamentals through the TOGAF framework. It covers distinctions between TOGAF 9.2 and TOGAF 10, core concepts defining enterprise architecture, and the Architecture Development Method (ADM). The program examines architecture governance, stakeholder management, and related methodologies essential for practitioners. The course is structured across three main modules covering foundational concepts, ADM phases, and governance practices, with approximately 6 hours of instructional content.

- [Enterprise Data Architecture and Operations](https://www.coursera.org/learn/enterprise-data-architecture-and-operations): This course provides foundational knowledge of Enterprise Data Architecture (EDA), exploring core components including data models and industry-standard frameworks such as TOGAF and the Zachman Framework. It enables learners to design architecture that meets specific business requirements and understand how data architecture supports business strategy and operations.

- [Build a Business Architecture using AWS Organization](https://www.coursera.org/projects/build-a-business-architecture-using-aws-organization): This guided project teaches how to manage business architecture deployed in AWS cloud using AWS Organization, a powerful tool for managing multiple AWS Accounts. The hands-on project provides practical experience in implementing business architecture in cloud environments, demonstrating how architectural principles translate to modern cloud infrastructure.

## Udemy courses

- [Business Architecture Foundation with TOGAF 10 Standard](https://www.udemy.com/course/togaf-10-ba-foundation/): This comprehensive course covers all concepts needed for the TOGAF Business Architecture Foundation Exam, based on the latest TOGAF Standard 10th Edition. Created by Dr. Christopher Schulz and Tobias Smuda, the course includes comprehensible and visual learning material, official learning goals, and exam questions to prepare for certification.

- [Enterprise Architecture with TOGAF Standard 10th Edition](https://www.udemy.com/course/togaf-10-certification-enterprise-and-business-architecture/): This course offers comprehensive coverage of TOGAF 10th Edition, focusing on enterprise and business architecture with strategic alignment. It includes almost 6 hours of video content, 90 supporting materials, interactive quizzes, and two full-length practice exams. The course is designed for aspiring architects, IT professionals, and business strategists seeking to align technology with business goals, covering all four key architecture domains: business, data, application, and technology.

- [Practicing TOGAF 10 and E.A. Modeling with the Archi Tool](https://www.udemy.com/course/practicing-togaf-10-and-ea-modelling-with-the-archi-tool/): This practical course combines TOGAF 10 theory with hands-on enterprise architecture modeling using the Archi tool, an open-source ArchiMate modeling platform. The course provides real-world application of TOGAF concepts through modeling exercises, helping learners translate architectural concepts into visual models that can be used for stakeholder communication.

### Skillsoft courses

The content covers how to develop architectures that effectively bridge strategic intent and operational reality.

- [Enterprise Architecture Basics - TOGAF 9](https://www.skillsoft.com/course/enterprise-architecture-basics-caba2cb8-e179-11e6-93f3-0242c0a80605): This course explores the fundamentals of the TOGAF framework for enterprise architecture, focusing on enterprise framework concepts, architecture types, and the development method. It provides foundational knowledge essential for understanding how enterprise architecture supports business transformation and strategic objectives.

- [Architecture Development Method (ADM) Cycle - TOGAF 9](https://www.skillsoft.com/course/architecture-development-method-adm-cycle-d4cd2224-292d-11e7-9844-0242c0a8080b): This advanced course helps design, plan, implement, and govern enterprise IT architecture through an in-depth explanation of the TOGAF framework and Enterprise Architecture. It focuses specifically on the ADM cycle, which is the core method for developing and managing enterprise architectures through iterative phases from architecture vision to change management.

## Blog posts

- [Business Architecture Strategy: Building a Strong Foundation for Organizational Success](https://bizzdesign.com/blog/business-architecture-strategy): Published by Bizzdesign in September 2025, this post emphasizes creating detailed models and diagrams to visualize the future state and facilitate stakeholder communication. It recommends involving stakeholders early in the process, communicating benefits clearly, and providing training and support to overcome resistance to change. The article stresses fostering strong stakeholder engagement by involving them in planning and decision-making.

- [3 Trends Driving Enterprise Architecture Strategy in 2025](https://www.gartner.com/en/articles/2025-trends-for-enterprise-architecture): Published by Gartner in August 2025, this article explains that EA leaders must develop a new EA operating model to deliver value to organizations that have embraced federated business designs. The article emphasizes the need to continuously communicate the value they deliver to stakeholders and internal customers in evolving organizational structures.

- [Enterprise Architecture Strategy: The Ultimate Guide for 2025](https://www.superblocks.com/blog/enterprise-architecture-strategy): Published by Superblocks in March 2025, this comprehensive guide highlights having conversations with key stakeholders, noting you can't build an effective architecture if you don't know what the leadership team is prioritizing. It provides practical guidance on aligning architecture initiatives with leadership priorities and organizational goals.

- [Enterprise Architecture as Strategy: Foundation for Business Execution](https://www.mega.com/blog/strategy-enterprise-architecture): Published by MEGA in November 2024, this post discusses how EA facilitates effective communication and stakeholder alignment using standardized architectural frameworks and models. It explores how enterprise architecture serves as a foundation for executing business strategy through structured approaches and standardized communication methods.

- [The Power of Enterprise Architecture: Turning Business Strategy into Reality](https://www.valueblue.com/blog/the-power-of-enterprise-architecture-turning-business-strategy-into-reality): This ValueBlue article explains that when equipped with the right methods, Enterprise Architecture can create better alignment between business strategy and infrastructure, ensure stakeholder buy-in and communication, and lay a solid foundation for continuous improvement. It emphasizes the practical application of EA in bridging strategy and execution.

- [The Business Architect's Toolkit: Essential Models and Frameworks](https://enterprisearchitecture.work/blog/2024/the-business-architects-toolkit-essential-models-and-frameworks/): Published in February 2024, this resource covers essential business architecture models including business process modeling, enterprise architecture, capability modeling, and value stream mapping. It provides practical guidance on when and how to use different architectural tools and frameworks effectively.

- [UK Government Digital and Data Profession Capability Framework - Business Architect](https://ddat-capability-framework.service.gov.uk/business-architect.html): Updated on February 28, 2025, this official UK government framework defines the business architect role, including updates to the 'stakeholder relationship management' skill to improve clarity. It describes how a business architect develops an integrated view of an enterprise and uses this view to help their organisation put its strategy in place.

- [The Business Architect's Toolbox: Value Stream Mapping](https://bizzdesign.com/blog/business-architect-toolbox-stream-mapping): Published by Bizzdesign in September 2025, this post describes Value Stream Mapping (VSM) as "one of the most powerful tools in a business architect's arsenal" that helps organizations "identify inefficiencies, align capabilities, and drive strategic improvements". The article discusses the four core domains of business architecture: Value Streams, Capabilities, Organization, and Information.

- [TOGAF Explained: An Enterprise Architecture Methodology](https://www.advisedskills.com/blog/enterprise-architecture/togaf-explained-an-enterprise-architecture-methodology): This comprehensive guide explains TOGAF's decision-making framework and how it "helps organizations be more transparent" and "reduces biases and encourages accountability among decision-makers". It covers the six main characteristics of governance including discipline, transparency, and reasoned decision-making guided by different architecture disciplines.

- [The Role of Business Architects in Strategic Planning](https://www.capstera.com/business-architects-in-strategic-planning/): This article explains how Business Architects specialize in designing and optimizing an organization's structure, processes, and capabilities with a deep understanding of business strategy, technology, and operations. They partner in the strategic planning process by providing input to the scope definition of strategic initiatives leveraging capabilities, creating capability Heatmaps to provide visibility to impacts, dependencies, overlaps, and opportunities.

## Research articles

- [Enterprise Business Architecture as a Tool for Sustainable Development in an Enterprise - Case Study](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1877050921020226): This ScienceDirect article presents a case study examining how Enterprise Business Architecture serves as a tool for sustainable development. It demonstrates practical applications of business architecture principles in real organizational contexts and their impact on long-term sustainability and strategic alignment.

- [Enterprise Architecture as Strategy - Creating a Foundation for Business Execution](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/236972734_Enterprise_Architecture_as_Strategy_-_Creating_a_Foundation_for_Business_Execution): This seminal research paper on ResearchGate explores how enterprise architecture serves as the foundation for business execution. It examines the relationship between EA and business strategy, demonstrating how architectural approaches can transform strategic intent into operational reality.

- [Business Strategy Modelling Based on Enterprise Architecture: A State of the Art Review](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/325857119_Business_strategy_modelling_based_on_enterprise_architecture_a_state_of_the_art_review): Published in Business Process Management Journal (Vol. 25, No. 4, pp. 606-624), this systematic literature review examines how business strategy can be modeled using enterprise architecture approaches. It provides comprehensive coverage of academic research in this area, analyzing trends and best practices.

- [The Benefits of Enterprise Architecture in Organizational Transformation](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s12599-019-00605-3): Published in Business & Information Systems Engineering (Springer, 2019-2020), this article demonstrates how Enterprise Architecture (EA) has been widely adapted as a planning and governance approach to manage complexity and constant change, and to align the organization toward a common goal. EA products support decision-making and communication, strategic management, transformation governance, and IT and business planning activities.

- [Digital Transformation in Higher Education: An ArchiMate-Based Business Architecture Case Study](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/387359287_Digital_Transformation_in_Higher_Education_An_ArchiMate-based_Business_Architecture_Case_Study): Published in IEEE Access (2024), this study explores digital transformation within a university, utilizing the ArchiMate modeling language from a business architecture perspective. The research uses design science methodology, qualitative and inductive methods with ArchiMate to offer theoretical insights and practical guidelines for institutions undergoing transformations.

- [Exploring Business Strategy Modelling with ArchiMate: A Case Study Approach](https://www.mdpi.com/2078-2489/13/1/31): Published in MDPI Information journal (2022), this study shows how modeling company strategy using ArchiMate supported managers in understanding and aligning elements such as organizational vision, mission, goals, resources, and capabilities with competitive advantage. The article facilitates knowledge from strategic planning and enterprise architecture research areas.

- [Exploring Capabilities for Digital Transformation in the Business Context: Insight from a Systematic Literature Review](https://www.mdpi.com/2071-1050/17/9/4222): Published in Sustainability (2025), this systematic literature review identified capabilities essential for digital transformation in business contexts through analysis of 43 relevant articles following the PRISMA methodology. The study revealed five core dimensions of digital transformation capability: digital dynamic capability, digital leadership capability, employee digital capability, digital technology and operational capability, and digital investment capability.

- [Behavioral Strategy and the Strategic Decision Architecture of the Firm](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/0008125617712256): Published in California Management Review by Olivier Sibony, Dan Lovallo, and Thomas C. Powell, this research proposes a typology of strategic decision processes in organizations and shows how managers can use these processes to design a behaviorally-informed decision architecture of the firm. It demonstrates how behavioral strategy has made significant inroads in academic research.

- [Enterprise Architecture Governance: The Need for a Business-to-IT Approach](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/44939627_Enterprise_Architecture_Governance_The_Need_for_a_Business-to-IT_Approach): This research paper examines how the management processes and control mechanisms of IT governance are inadequate for holistic governance of enterprise architecture. Whereas IT governance primarily focuses on running daily IT operations, EA is primarily focused on designing the future state of architecture in support of business objectives.

- [Business Process Modeling: Perceived Benefits](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-642-04840-1_34): This Springer chapter explores the perception of benefits derived from process modeling initiatives through a global Delphi study incorporating the views of three groups of stakeholders – academics, practitioners, and vendors. It demonstrates how business process modeling workflow analysis has the potential not only to achieve competitive advantage through customer satisfaction but also to help sustain relationships with different stakeholders.

- [Introduction to Enterprise Infrastructure, Software Architecture, Zachman Framework, and TOGAF](https://videohighlight.com/v/H1eDjr6tx5U): This video provides a comprehensive introduction to enterprise infrastructure and major architecture frameworks. The Zachman framework consists of six viewpoints based on the five W's (Who, What, Where, When, Why) and one H (How), which guide stakeholder involvement in system implementation. Stakeholders are categorized into groups such as planners, owners, designers, implementers, subcontractors, and users, with each group representing different viewpoints within the organizational structure.

## Books

It provides practical guidance on creating architectures that serve as foundations for business execution and competitive advantage. Jeanne W. Ross, Peter Weill, David Robertson.

- [The TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition - Business Architecture - 2025 Update](https://amazon.com/TOGAF®-Standard-10th-Business-Architecture-ebook/dp/B0FFZYH5FT): The official 2025 update to the TOGAF Standard covering business architecture. This authoritative resource covers business modeling, business capabilities, value streams, information mapping, and TOGAF business scenarios. Essential reading for business architects seeking to understand the latest TOGAF standards and best practices. The Open Group.

- [Strategic Enterprise Architecture Management: Challenges, Best Practices, and Future Developments](https://www.amazon.com/Strategic-Enterprise-Architecture-Management-Professionals-ebook/dp/B00B3PHM6Q): This book explains how top executives can use EAM for leveraging their strategic planning and controlling processes, based on analysis of best practices from eight leading European companies. It provides research-based insights into effective enterprise architecture management approaches and their impact on organizational performance. Frederik Ahlemann, Eric Stettiner, Marcus Messerschmidt, Christine Legner.

- [Enterprise Architecture at Work: Modelling, Communication and Analysis](https://www.amazon.com/Enterprise-Architecture-Work-Communication-Engineering-ebook/dp/B073FNZKM9): This fourth edition presents techniques and heuristics for communicating with all relevant stakeholders about architectures. - [Enterprise Architecture: A Pragmatic Guide For Practitioners & Stakeholders](https://www.amazon.com/Enterprise-Architecture-Pragmatic-Practitioners-Stakeholders/dp/B0FFT7ZF8N): This pragmatic guide reveals how organizations can achieve their objectives through pragmatic models and project initiatives, empowering stakeholders to drive meaningful transformation. Gerben Wierda.

- [The Business Architecture Quick Guide: A Brief Guide for GameChangers](https://www.amazon.com/Business-Architecture-Quick-Guide-GameChangers-ebook/dp/B079ZBD75L): This quick guide addresses how business architecture practice is used to drive business and IT transformation in organizations and institutions. Business Architecture Guild.

- [Business Architecture: A Practical Guide](https://www.amazon.com/Business-Architecture-Practical-Jonathan-Whelan/dp/1138247316): Written by experienced practitioners (Jonathan Whelan with experience as Consultant, Strategist, Business Architect and Programme Manager, and Graham Meaden who headed up the global Strategic Architecture and Business Architecture teams for HSBC Group), this practical guide provides real-world insights into business architecture practice and implementation. Jonathan Whelan, Graham Meaden.

Karen Martin, Mike Osterling.

## Professional development tips

- Commit to continuous learning because tools and techniques evolve rapidly
- Build a portfolio of demonstration projects so you can share your skills
- Join relevant communities, attend events, and connect with colleagues
- Practice explaining concepts to audiences in person and also in writing
- Focus on developing both technical skills and business acumen
- Seek opportunities to work with diverse people and projects
